Mission

Academically empowering young scholars through and social justice through a cultural relevant creative literacy program. This multilayered program provides enrichments and interventions for students in literacy in addition to a hip hop cultured background.

Vision

To prepare students to be academically, socially and emotionally ready for student achievement.

Purpose

This work needs to be done! Research supports a variety of most hip hop based literacy programs.There is not much research that supports an innovative curriculum program infused with the culture of hip hop and the foundations of reading and writing.

    Hip Hop Literacy

    A research based Hip Hop literacy curriculum program that influences reading and writing through empowerment, social justice through culturally relevant teaching and learning. It’s a six step reading program that incorporates the 5 elements of reading along with a writing component.

    • Alphabet Chant/Rap (Alliteration/Onset/LN, LS)
    • Rhyme, Rap and Read (CVC words)
    • Piece It and Release it (letter combinations, 1 sounds)
    • Word Work
    • Pre/post test

    My philosophy is to teach with a creative heart that leds students down a path of “cracking the code” of reading in a fun and innovative way.
